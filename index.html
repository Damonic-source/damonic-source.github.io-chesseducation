About Chess

Chess is a two-player strategy board game played on an 8x8 grid. It is one of the most popular games in the world.

Rules of Chess

The objective of the game is to checkmate your opponent's king. Each piece has its own unique way of moving.

  • King: The king can move one square in any direction.
  • Queen: The queen is the most powerful piece and can move any number of squares in any direction.
  • Rook: The rook can move any number of squares horizontally or vertically.
  • Bishop: The bishop can move any number of squares diagonally.
  • Knight: The knight moves in an L-shape (two squares in one direction, then one square to the side).
  • Pawn: The pawn can move forward one square, but captures diagonally one square.
